ChoreTracker Pivots
Reward System

The first pivot our team made was based on a suggestion from Professor Gessner: adding a rewawrds system. We verified its interest through customer surveys, which strongly indicated a desire to motivate their kids with rewards. We chose a point system because it allows the parents to decide what the kids can redeem their points for.

Central Hub

The second pivot was to remove the raspberry pi central hub and have the sensors send data directly to our server. This would greatly cut costs and puts us in position for a future pivot: integrate our sensors with existing hubs, such as Amazon Alexa and Google Home. That would let us leverage products many people already have and use them for reliable transmission of data from the sensors.

Pivot Types
Reward System

This pivot is a Customer Need Pivot. It came from directly identifying the needs of our existing customers and making a change to better suit those needs.

Central Hub

The central hub change was primarily a Technology Pivot. Based on the popularity of the existing home products (like Alexa), we think eventually integrating our product with those will be a strong improvement to the customer experience, while lowering our costs.
