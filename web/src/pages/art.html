Like most artists, I began drawing at a very early age. I started with traditional drawings not made using a computerart, eventually specializing in black and white ink. Then in 2020, I purchased my first drawing tablet and began digital art. Nowadays, I don't get to draw as much as I'd like, but when I do, it's usually digital. This page only holds a fraction of my artworks, but they are some of my favorites.
