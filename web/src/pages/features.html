Features vs Benefits
Features
  1. Parents being able to organize chores for a househould and assign them to kids directly
  2. Kids reporting when they finish their chores by marking it completed on the app
  3. Point system rewarding kids upon the completion of their chores, when verified by parents
  4. Sensors verifying whether the task was completed by the child when they marked it done
  5. Sensors remind parents and kids when chores need to be done (ex: "kitchen trash is full")
Benefits
  1. The task of organizing chores is made easier for parents
  2. It's easy for kids to push a button to mark the completion of a task
  3. Kids are motivated to do their chores so they can get rewards
  4. Parents ensuring chores are done is a lot easier
  5. Forgetting about chores will no longer be an issue
MVP Key Interactions
Software
  1. The "add child" functionality will allow parents to create users for their family
  2. The "add chore" interaction will enable parents to assign any chore to ther kids
  3. Kids see their chores on their screen and click the complete button for each chore they finish
  4. Parents can see live updates on the chores verified by sensors by clicking on those chores
Hardware
  1. The sensors will be easy to attach, almost like a sticker
  2. The sensors won't need their batteries changed often, and it will be a simple process
