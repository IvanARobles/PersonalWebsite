The easiest and safest way to get your kids to do their chores.
Key Value Proposition Meet the Team
The ChoreTracker is a collection of sensors that work together with our app to make organizing, assigning, and tracking chores easy.
  • Create recurring or one-time chores
  • Monitor chores and receive real-time updates
  • Reward your kids for a job well done
Who can use the Chore Tracker?
The ChoreTracker can be used by anyone, but it's especially useful for you busy parents. When kids don't get their chores done, it's parents who pay the price. With ChoreTracker, you quickly designate tasks and have a reward system of points to motivate kids for doing them. Then you simply give them screen time, allowances, or whatever you want for the points they have earned.
Benefits

Adults benefit because we take the hassle out of organizing chores

Kids benefit from having clear directions and rewards as motivation

How does it work?
It's as easy as 1.. 2.. 3..

Install the app and easily attach your sensors to your appliances.
